How to Delete Your RongDong Account

To delete your RongDong account and all associated data:

  1. Open the RongDong app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your choice

If you're unable to delete through the app, email us at: rongdongsupport@gmail.com

Data deleted: All posts, comments, profile info, and account data.

Data retained: None.
