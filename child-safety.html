Child Safety Standards - RongDong

Last updated: April 18, 2026

RongDong is a social media platform intended for users aged 18 and above. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that exploits or harms minors.

Our Commitments

  • We do not allow minors (under 18) to create accounts.
  • Any CSAM content is immediately removed and reported to authorities.
  • Users can report harmful content via the in-app reporting feature.
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement regarding child safety.
  • We regularly monitor content to ensure compliance with child safety standards.

Reporting

If you find any content that violates our child safety policy, please report it immediately through the app or contact us directly.
