🎨 RongDong - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, such as your name, email address, and profile photo.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the RongDong app experience, including posts, comments, and social features.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the app.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Camera Permission

RongDong requires camera access for the following features:

  • Profile Picture: To upload or change your profile photo.
  • Video Upload: To record and share videos directly within the app.
  • Live Streaming: To broadcast live video to other users.

Camera access is only requested when you actively use these features. We do not access your camera without your explicit permission. You can deny or revoke camera access at any time from your device settings.

6. User Rights

You can request to delete your account and data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

RongDong is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
